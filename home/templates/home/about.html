About Us

Pasty Bites is an online local pastry shop ran by Cigdem Askin, based in El Dorado Hills near Sacramento, California. Pastry Bites is known for their baklava, a Turkish pastry made of filo pastry, chopped nuts, and syrup or honey.

Our baklava is freshly baked by Cigdem Askin, and pickup will be arranged locally after seamless transaction.

Our goal is to make sure that customers near the Sacramento area are satisfied and taken care of, and can enjoy our delicious baklava!
